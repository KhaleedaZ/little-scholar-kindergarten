Upcoming Event: Nashid Competition

Date: 03 October 2021
Venue: Kuala Lumpur City Hall
Time : 9.00 a.m - 5.00 p.m

Nashid competition is a nashid singing competition which conducted at Kuala Lumpur City Hall. The competition is consist of 6-7 teams competing in Nashid singing in Arabic. Each team will have 5-6 members. This competition usually for Muslim Little Scholar students although non-Muslim competitors can also join. Parents can come to the competition to support their children.

The judges evaluate their performance based on these criteria:

  • 40% for voice quality and performance
  • 30% for lyric
  • 30% for melody

The champion will receive RM 300, medals and hampers; runner-up will receive RM 200, medals and hampers and 3rd place will receive RM 100 and hampers.
